3. What We Collect:

We collect the following categories of personal data to offer our services:

  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number.
  • Identification Details: Verified by a third-party API (QoreID.com).
  • Location Data: Used to cluster users based on proximity for easy meetups.
  • User-generated content: Messages, photos, and other information shared during user interactions on the platform.
  • Behavioral data: Information about user preferences, likes, and interactions within the app.
  • Social media information: If you link your social media accounts to Intwit, we may collect data like profile information, friend lists, or interests.
  • Technical and Device Information: Information about the device you use, including IP address, device identifiers, and browser type.

4. How We Collect Your Data:

We collect your personal data in the following ways:

  • Directly from you: When you register on the app or website, provide information, or use features like messaging or location-based services.
  • Device Permissions: We request permission to access your camera, microphone, contacts, and location for enhanced functionality of the app.

5. Legal Basis for Processing:

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For features such as location-based services, social media connections, and personalized marketing, we obtain explicit consent. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Performance of a Contract: We process your data to provide the services you sign up for and to fulfil our contractual obligations.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations in Nigeria or Europe.
  • Legitimate Interests: We rely on legitimate interests to improve our services and user experience, prevent fraud, and ensure the security of our platform. Where legitimate interest is the legal basis, we ensure that this does not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.
